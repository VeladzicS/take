Description

This is one of the most beautiful tours on the mountain Visocica. It is a bit more demanding, but apart from good condition, it does not require any special mountaineering skills and equipment. It is only not recommended for people who are afraid of heights. The tour can be realized throughout the year. In the winter months, when the ridge is covered with snow, additional equipment is needed (ice ax, crampons, mountaineering belt and rope) and the difficulty of the tour is much higher and is recommended only to more experienced mountaineers who know how to use the equipment.


The tour starts at the mountain lodge Tušila, then along the path through the forest to the amphitheater which consists of the peaks Vito (1960 m) and Subar (1820 m). From there, a rather strong ascent to the top of Vito begins, and then a beautiful ridge leads to the top of Drstvo (1808 m). All the way you have a view of the rest of Visocica, then Bjelasnica, Treskavica, all the way to Prenja and Čvrsnica. We can guarantee that crossing this ridge will stay in your memory for a long time.
